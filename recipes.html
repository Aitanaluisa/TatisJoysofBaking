Homemade Cinnamon rolls (8 large rolls)

    Dough Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk (milk temp should be 110F)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup sour cream, room temp
  • 1/2 cup salted butter (very soft)
  • 2 eggs, room temp
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 4 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 1 tsp salt

    • Cinnamon filling:

  • 1/2 cup salted butter, room temp
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream (to pour over the rolls)

    • Cream Cheese frosting:

  • 8oz cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste

    • Instructions:

  • Activate the yeast. Pour the wamr milk into a small bowl. Add 1 tsp of the granulated sugar and add the yeast overtop. Stir and let sit for 10 mins until it is frothy and activated. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Set aside
  • Once it is activated, pour the yeast mixture into the bowl of a stand mixer. Add the rest of the sugar, sour cream, butter, eggs, vanilla and the flour.
  • Using the dough hook attachment, mix on low on speed 1, until mixure is roughly combined. Let rest for 5 mins so the flour can soak up the liquid. After 5 mins, mix on medium speed for 5-7 mins until the dough is a smooth, elastic and pulling aways from the sides of the bowl. If the dough seems too sticky, you can add 1 tsp of flour at a time until it pulls away from the side of the bowl.The dough should be slightly sticky!
  • Move the dough into a lightly greased bowl.Cover and let rise in a warm environment for 30-45 mins or until the dough has doubled in size. While the dough is rising, prepare the cinnamon filling by mixing the butter,brown sugar,cinnamon, and the vanilla. Set aside.
  • Once the dough is done rising and doubled in size, flour your work surface. Roll the dough out to 16x15 rectangle. Spread and smooth out the filling mixutre evenly on the dough.
  • Start on the long end and tightly roll the dough into a log. Using a sharp knife, cut the dough into 8 large rolls. Rolls should be about 2in wide. Place the rolls into the prepared baking pan. Cover the rolls and proof for another 30 mins until puffy. Preheat the oven to 350F. Once the rolls are done proofing warm up the heavy whipping cream and pour over the rolls. Bake in the oven for 25 mins until the tops are golden brown and the rolls are cooked through.
  • While the rolls cool down, prepare the cream cheese frosting. Mix the softened cream cheese and butter until smooth and fluffy. Then add the powdered sugar and vanilla until smooth and well combined. Spread the cream cheese frosting onto the rolls. Serve and Enjoy!
